Our services

Water Safety & Rescue Services

Colwick Park Lifeguards bring over 30 years of expertise to ensure the safety of your open water events. From festivals and triathlons to film and TV productions, our dedicated lifeguards and safety boats are equipped to handle diverse water safety needs.

At the core of our services are qualified lifeguards, first aiders, and skilled safety boat helms. With a commitment to professionalism, we prioritize the well-being of everyone in, on, and near open water.

Our fleet

Our impressive fleet includes rigid inflatables (RIBs) of various sizes, hard-shelled boats, fully inflatable boats, Piranha Dorys, paddle craft, and an off-road vehicle. Each vessel is strategically chosen to adapt to different water conditions, providing flexibility for various events.

Specialist Equipment

Equipped with specialist rescue gear, including first aid kits, radios, and dry suits, our fleet ensures we are prepared for any situation. Safety is our top priority, and our gear reflects our commitment to excellence.

Our fleet

Our fleet consists of a range of different boats, specialist equipeent and vehicles, suited to a wide range of applications.

Rigid Inflatable Boats (RIBs)

Possibly our most versatile boats, RIBs can reliably tackle a wide range of conditions, while providing a very stable platform to perform rescues from, and capacity for many casualties.

Small Inflatable Boats

Our smaller inflatable boats are especially suited to smaller lakes and festival sites, they are easy to transport across sites and can be launched without a slipway. They can also operate in shallower waters, and can be rowed if deployed to an area where an outboard engine would not be suitable.

Piranha Dory's

These are solid hulled boats with a large open deck. They provide ample room for treating a casualty on scene, and even have enough space to lie a spinal board flat.

Paddle Craft

We have a selection of surk ski's, canoes and kayaks which we rely on heavily for open water swimming and similar activities.

Off Road Vehicle

Our 4x4 is not only used to tow and launch our rescue boats, but also as a base at events where our lifeguards operate from the land. It can provided a point of contact for members of the public and can be set up as a lifeguarding position and first aid point.

Specialist Rescue Equipment

All of our fleet are kitted out with specialist rescue equipment, including rescue aids, first aid kits, radios, dry suits for staff and more.
